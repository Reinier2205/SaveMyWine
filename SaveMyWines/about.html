About SaveMyWines

Our Mission

SaveMyWines helps wine enthusiasts build and manage their personal wine collections. Whether you're a casual wine drinker or a serious collector, our app makes it easy to keep track of the wines you've enjoyed and discover new favorites.

How It Works

  • Scan Labels: Use your phone's camera to scan wine labels and automatically add them to your collection.
  • Manual Entry: Prefer to type? Add wines manually with our simple form.
  • Organize: Sort and filter your wines by varietal, region, vintage, and more.
  • Personal Notes: Add tasting notes, ratings, and memories to each wine.

Privacy & Security

We believe your wine collection is personal. That's why SaveMyWines stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information or wine data.
